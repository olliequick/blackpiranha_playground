Sparrowhawk

A modern, privacy-focused, and highly customizable RSS reader for Android. Built for readers who value speed, simplicity, and control.

View on GitHub

Why Sparrowhawk?

📖 Premium Reading

Full preservation of article formatting with professional typography and smart navigation that prevents annoying list jumps.

🛠️ Powerful Management

Automated feed discovery—just paste a website URL. Organize your sources with custom categories and OPML support.

🎨 Deep Customization

Material 3 interface with Light and Dark modes. Choose your favorite Google Fonts for a truly personal reading experience.

🧹 Privacy First

All data stays on your device. Local Room database storage and foreground-only maintenance preserve your battery and privacy.

Meet the Developer

Hi! I'm the creator of Sparrowhawk. I built this app because I wanted an RSS reader that respected my privacy while providing a high-quality reading experience that felt native to modern Android.

My goal is to keep Sparrowhawk fast, lightweight, and open-source.
